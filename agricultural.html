Agriculture Services

Import of Crops

The import of crops involves bringing agricultural products from other countries into the domestic market. This is often done to meet the demand for crops that are not locally grown,out of season. Importing crops can enhance food security,diversify diets and provide ingredients necessary for various food industries.

Crop Export

Crop exportation is the selling and shipping of domestically grown agricultural products to international markets. It helps farmers and agricultural businesses access broader markets,increase their income and contribute to the national economy. Grains,fruits,vegetables and cash crops like coffee and cotton are frequently major export crops.

Invention of Modern Rice

The invention of modern rice,particularly high-yielding varieties was significantly advanced by the Green Revolution in the mid-20th century. Scientists like Dr. Norman Borlaug developed new strains of rice that were disease-resistant,had shorter growing cycles and produced higher yields.

Information on Agriculture Practices

Agricultural practices enclose the methods and techniques used in farming to enhance productivity and sustainability. Crop rotation,organic farming,pesticide and fertilizer use,irrigation and cutting-edge technology like genetic engineering and precision farming are a few of these. The goals of good agricultural practices are to protect the environment,increase productivity and guarantee food safety.

Farmer Training

Farmer training programs are designed to educate and empower farmers with the latest knowledge and skills in agriculture. These programs cover various topics such as crop management, sustainable practices, use of technology, financial management, and market access. Effective training helps farmers increase productivity, adopt innovative practices, and improve their livelihoods.
