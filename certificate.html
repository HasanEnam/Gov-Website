Certificate Services

Death Certificates

A death certificate is an official document issued by a government authority that records the date,location and cause of a person's death. It is essential for legal purposes,including the settlement of estates,cessation of social services and insurance claims. The certificate typically includes personal details of the deceased.

Freedom Fighter Certificates

A freedom fighter certificate is an official recognition awarded to individuals who participated in a country's struggle for independence or significant political movements. This certificate acknowledges their contributions and sacrifices made during the freedom movement. It often entitles recipients to certain benefits,honors and pensions as a token of gratitude from the nation.

Medical Center Certificates

A medical center certificate is an official document provided by a medical institution verifying a person's health status or medical treatment. This can include fitness for work,vaccination status,results of medical examinations or proof of illness. It is used for various purposes such as employment,travel,school admission or legal matters.

Passport Copy Certificates

A passport copy certificate is an authenticated copy of a person's passport certified by a notary or relevant authority. It is often used when the original passport cannot be submitted or must be retained by the owner. This certified copy is used for various legal,financial or travel-related processes where proof of identity and nationality is required.

Birth Certificates

A birth certificate is an official document issued by a government authority that records the birth of a child. It includes details such as the child's name,date,place of birth and the names of the parents. This certificate serves as a primary proof of identity and age and is required for many essential activities such as enrolling in school,obtaining a passport and applying for government benefits.
