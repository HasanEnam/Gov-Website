Letter of Introduction

A letter of introduction is a document used to introduce one person to another or a business to a potential client or partner. It outlines who you are,your purpose and any relevant background information,facilitating a new connection or relationship. It's commonly used in professional settings to establish credibility and initiate communication.

Request for Information

A request for information is a formal inquiry used to gather details about products,services or policies from another organization. It help in understanding options,capabilities and market conditions.

Department of ICT

The Department of ICT is a governmental or organizational body responsible for the implementation,maintenance and regulation of information and communication technologies. This department oversees policies related to internet access,cybersecurity and the promotion of technology-driven innovation.

Loom Factory

A loom factory is a manufacturing facility where looms are used to weave fabrics from yarn. These factories produce a variety of textiles such as cloth,carpets and other woven materials. The operations typically involve both traditional mechanical looms and modern automated weaving machines to meet the demands of different markets.

Remittance Worker

A remittance worker is an individual who works abroad and sends a portion of their earnings back to their home country. These remittances are a vital source of income for many families and can significantly contribute to the economic stability and development of their home regions. Remittance workers often support their families and communities by providing funds for essential needs such as housing,education and healthcare.
